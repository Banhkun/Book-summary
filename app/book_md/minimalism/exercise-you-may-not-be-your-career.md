## Exercise: You May Not Be Your Career

Many people invest themselves in their careers to the point of assuming an identity and social status based on their job title. When their career ends, they lack a sense of purpose.

* * *

How do you define yourself, or what is your identity?

If your identity is tied to your career or occupation, what other, more meaningful labels could you adopt that can apply no matter what you do? (Examples include mentor, grandparent, teacher, artist, **____** enthusiast.)
