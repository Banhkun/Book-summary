## Exercise: Where Do You Stand?

Think through Thaler and Sunstein’s provocative nudges.

* * *

Do you think patients should be allowed to waive their rights to sue for medical malpractice? Why or why not? Would _you_ waive your rights to sue for a discount?

Reflect on Thaler and Sunstein’s marriage reform. Should states get out of the marriage business altogether? If you’re currently married, would you be comfortable with a civil union rather than marriage? Why or why not?
