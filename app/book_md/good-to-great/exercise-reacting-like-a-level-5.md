## Exercise: Reacting Like a Level 5

Answer the following questions to learn what kind of leader you are.

* * *

Think of a recent success, whether at your job or in your personal life. Describe the success below.

Now explore your _reaction_ to that success. Were you quick to _take_ credit or _deflect_ it? Did you boast about your success or take it in stride?

Now think of a recent setback, and describe that setback below.

Explore your _reaction_ to the setback. Were you quick to _take_ blame or _deflect_ it? What have you _learned_ , either about your work or yourself, from the setback?
