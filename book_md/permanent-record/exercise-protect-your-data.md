## Exercise: Protect Your Data

Encryption is one of the only ways to protect yourself against surveillance.

* * *

What programs, apps, and devices do you use on a regular basis?

Which ones are the most secure, and which ones are the least secure? How do you know? (Hint: Look for their policy on encryption and check the terms and conditions.)

How could you make your data more secure? Consider replacing unencrypted apps with encrypted ones (for example, switch to Signal for texts and call).
