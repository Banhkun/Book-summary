## Exercise: Prepare for the Worst

Learn from others’ mistakes and act cautiously to avoid losing big.

* * *

Describe the last situation or project where you didn’t prepare for the worst and ended up losing time, money, or progress.

Describe a recent situation when you acted cautiously and it paid off.

How can you prepare for the worst in your current project?

How could you build in a process to identify and mitigate risks at the start of your future projects?
