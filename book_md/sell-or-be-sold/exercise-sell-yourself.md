## Exercise: Sell Yourself

You need to sell yourself on your product before you can sell it to anyone else.

* * *

Consider a product you sell or would like to sell. What are some of its benefits?

What problems does it solve?

Do you already own it yourself? Why or why not?

If you don’t own it and don’t want to own it, you’re not sold enough. How could you strengthen your conviction?
