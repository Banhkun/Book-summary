## Exercise: Instituting ‘Deliberate Action’

To reduce errors made by people acting on autopilot, _Santa Fe_ used a procedure called taking “deliberate action.” Before acting, a crew member would pause and verbally state what he intended to do.

* * *

What kinds of mistakes have your employees made by acting on autopilot? How have you responded?

How have you and your company learned from mistakes?

How could you institute a “deliberate action” procedure to reduce errors in your company?
