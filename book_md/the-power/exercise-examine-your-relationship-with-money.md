## Exercise: Examine Your Relationship With Money

The thoughts and feelings we have about our finances determine our relationship with money. We can either become someone money sticks to or is repelled by.

* * *

What are your current financial goals?

What thoughts and feelings do you believe have kept you from achieving those goals before?

How can you activate the law of attraction with your thoughts and feelings to make your goals a reality?

What are two things you would do if your financial goals came true tomorrow?
